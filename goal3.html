Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being

Overview

This goal aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages.

Key Targets:

  • Reduce maternal mortality.
  • End epidemics of communicable diseases.
  • Achieve universal health coverage.

Importance:

Health is a precondition for development and poverty reduction, essential for achieving all other SDGs.
